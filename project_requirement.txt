There are two goals for this project:
1. create a product that allow user to collect company fundamental data, historical stock price data, social media data, and news data,
    and store them in a database or google spreadsheet. an ranking engine that uses the store data and user custom logic to rank company's 
    growth prospects in the following quarter after immediately after their earning release and conference call.
2. learn fundamentals of LLM, NLP and post-train fine-tuning of existing open source LLM, have a solid understanding of the Attention mechanism
    and the most important research papers that leads to the creation of chatGPT, and reasoning framework from Deepseek.
